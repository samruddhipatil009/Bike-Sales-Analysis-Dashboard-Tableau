# Bike-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Tableau

This Tableau dashboard analyzes bike store sales data from 2011-2016 across products, demographics, and geography to help businesses make data-driven decisions.

### Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard Screenshot](screenshots/dashboard_overview.png)

### Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: $20M+
- **Total Orders**: 10,000+
- **Time Period**: 2011 - 2016
- **Top Product**: Mountain Bikes - $8.2M
- **Top Markets**: United States & Australia

### Key Insights
1. **Demographics**: Peak sales in age group 35-54, contributing 42% of total revenue
2. **Products**: Mountain Bikes generated highest revenue, followed by Road Bikes
3. **Seasonality**: Q4 2015 had 23% YoY growth driven by holiday sales
4. **Geography**: United States & Australia were top 2 markets by sales volume
5. **Customer Segment**: Male customers contributed 52% of total sales
